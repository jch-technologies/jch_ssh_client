### Privacy

Last updated: January 2026

### Overview

JCH SSH Client processes data only to establish SSH connections configured by you and to provide terminal and file browser features. **No data is transmitted to servers of the developer.**

### What data is processed?

- **Connection data**: host, port, username, optional profiles
- **Authentication data**: password/passphrase input (only used to connect), and imported SSH key files
- **In-app usage data**: e.g. saved profiles

### Where is data stored?

- **Locally on your device** in the app sandbox.
- **Connection profiles** are stored locally (UserDefaults).
- **SSH key files** are stored locally inside the app sandbox after import and can be deleted inside the app.

### Network connections

The app only connects to SSH servers you specify. The developer has no access to:

- content of your SSH sessions
- transferred commands or files
- your authentication data
- your server addresses

### Tracking / analytics / advertising

The app does not use:

- analytics or tracking services (e.g. Google Analytics, Firebase)
- ad networks
- telemetry or usage statistics

### Deleting data

- Profiles: delete inside the app
- SSH keys: delete inside the app under key management
- Everything: uninstall the app
